What is a name?

A name is but an identifier, something to differentiate yourself from others. Over time; however, it becomes something greater. It becomes who you are; it becomes difficult to separate yourself from that name.

I have and always will be Anthony Nguyen, but that name doesn't quite satisfy the underlying reason for a name. It fails (at least more than other names) in giving me a unique identifier. I've been acquainted with more than just a few other Anthony Nguyens. Surprisingly, I've never met a John Smith.
